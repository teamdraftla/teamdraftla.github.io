Team Draft LA

Casual Magic the Gathering draft league in Los Angeles

League

Team Draft LA is a league that runs through the duration of every new Magic: the Gathering set with nominal entry fee of 75 per team going wholly towards prizing.

Team Draft

Team draft is a variation of draft, with 2 teams of 3 facing off against each other. The teams are seated in alternating order during draft, and will play round robin against the other team afterwards. The team that gets 5 wins first will win the match.

Draft

Draft is a method of playing Magic: the Gathering. Players will open a booster pack, choose a card, and pass the pack to the next player. This will repeat until all the cards are chosen. This will repeat for 3 packs when players will end up with 45 cards with which to build a 40 card deck.
